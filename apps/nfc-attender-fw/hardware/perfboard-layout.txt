LearnLife NFC Attender — Perfboard Layout (70x90mm, 0.1" pitch)
================================================================

Top-down view, looking at the component side. The board sits horizontally
in the bottom tray with its long axis (90mm) aligned LEFT-RIGHT in the
case. Front of the board faces the user.

Origin (0,0) = front-left corner of perfboard. "FRONT" faces the user.
Dimensions: 90mm along X (left-right), 70mm along Y (front-back).

ESP32 USB-C must face the BACK wall (where the case has its USB cutout).

Mounting holes: 3mm in from each corner, 2.8mm pilot for M2.5 self-tap.

Build order:
  1. Female header sockets first (warm-up joints).
  2. 3.3V rail (bare 24 AWG, soldered every other column along the back).
  3. GND rail (bare 24 AWG, soldered every other column along the back).
  4. SDA / SCL / buzzer GPIO traces (insulated 24 AWG).
  5. Flying leads (~80mm) for switch, button, USB-C 5V/GND.

The PN532 socket is offset to the right-back of the board so its antenna
coil sits directly beneath the "TAP CARD" label engraved on the top lid.
The OLED is on the left-back so it sits beneath the lid's display window.

Unused future expansion area: front-right of the board (room for an RGB
LED + current-limit resistors when added in v2).
